4.2 
Safety regulations 安全条例 
 
All applicable accident prevention rules must be observed.  
应当遵守各项事故预防规定。 
Potential risks caused by gases and vapours forming must be analysed in detail. The appropriate 
safety measures must be implemented.  
应当详细分析气体和蒸汽构成的潜在风险。应当实施适当的安全措施。 
Do not exceed permissible crane loads and weights on lifting gear and ropes/shackles. Secure 
loads against falling from a height.  
不要超过起重机和钢丝绳/吊具允许的起重载荷和重量。固定载荷，防止其从高处坠落。

threatening injuries and is thus strictly forbidden! 
切勿在悬吊的负荷下方站立或行走！由于站在悬吊的负荷下方可能导致危及生命的伤害，
因此严格禁止站立在悬吊的负荷下方！ 
Jolts must be avoided. This refers especially to the handling of pre-assembled machines.  
必须避免颠簸，尤其是在搬运预装配的设备时。 
 
Transport and unloading is to be carried out by personnel specially familiar with such work. 
应由熟悉此类工作的人员进行运输和卸料。 
Workers entrusted with lifting and conveying equipment must have the national qualifications re-
quired.  
负责吊装、搬运设备的工作人员，应当获得国家级资格证书。

4.4.1 
Safekeeping of rubberised rolls 安全存放施胶
辊筒 
 
Rubber-covered rolls should always be stored indoors, in their shipping crates. Rolls must be 
stored in an environment at temperatures above freezing point. 
施胶辊筒应始终存放在其运输包装内，室内保存。各种辊子必须在零度以上温度保存。 
A very cold roll should not be installed and put into service right away. It must be brought to the 
temperature at the installation location one or two days beforehand. 
温度非常低的辊子不得立即投入使用。必须提前一天或两天使其达到安装地点的温度。 
If rolls are to be stored for a longer period, the roll and the bearings should be rotated once a 
month to prevent the roll body from sagging and damage being caused to the bearings. 
如果辊子将要长时间存放，则应每月转动一次辊子和轴承，以防止辊体下垂并对轴承造成
损坏。 
Also observe the maintenance instructions of the rubberizing company! 
另请遵守施胶公司的维护说明！
