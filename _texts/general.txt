4.23.2 
Heating sequence of the Yankee 扬克缸加热
过程 
The cylinder must be heated manually first of all and under constant supervision. 
烘缸必须首先人工加热，并始终处在监督下。 
In order to check the surface temperature of the shell, a contact thermometer with a scale extend-
ing from 0 to at least 200°C should be used. 
为了检查外壳的表面温度，应使用刻度为0 到至少200°C 的接触式温度计。 
 
Steps 步骤 
a) Set Yankee in operation at approx. 30 m/min (crawl speed). 
将扬克缸设置在大约30 米/分钟速度运行（爬行速度）。 
b) Start the heating process in the distributed control system or, if the paper machine is controlled 
manually, open the steam valve according to the diagram and check the surface temperature as 
described under Section 7.3.1. 
在分散式控制系统内开始加热过程或如果造纸机是手动控制的，则按照图表打开蒸汽
阀，并检查7.3.1 节所述的表面温度。 
NOTICE! Too fast heating of the Yankee can lead to substantial differences in tempera-
ture and thus to damage to the Yankee! Heating progress should not exceed a maximum 
of 30°C/h! 
注意！扬克缸温升过快会导致温度的差异过大，从而会损坏扬克缸！温升进度最大不
应超过30°C/小时！ 
c) The pressure boost takes place if the condensate temperature in the Yankee reaches 90 °C. The 
pressure rises from 0 to 1 bar(g) in 1 hour. 
如果扬克缸中的冷凝水温度达到90℃，就会发生升压。压力在1 小时内从0 上升到1
巴（g）。 
d) Once the pressure of 1 bar (g) is reached, it needs 0.5 hours to reach a pressure of 2 bar (g). 
一旦达到1 巴（g）的压力，需要0.5 小时才能达到2 巴（g）的压力。 
e) Once a steam pressure of 2 bar has been reached (which corresponds to a surface 
temperature of approx. 120 °C), the Yankee is ready for operation. 
当蒸汽压力达到2 巴时（约相当于表面温度120°C），扬克缸已经准备好操作了。 
NOTICE! In order to confirm that the curve set in the control system is correct, the Yankee 
must be heated manually and under constant supervision during the first repeat heating pro-
cess! 
注意！为了确认控制系统中设定的曲线是正确的，在第一次重复加热过程中，扬克缸必须
在始终监督下手动加热！
