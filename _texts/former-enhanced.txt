--- 3.2.2 Breast roll (2) and swing device / 胸辊（2）和摆动装置 --- [Page 13]
FUNCTION: The raising/lowering device is used to swing the breast roll (2) upwards.
功能：升降装置用于将胸辊（2）向上摆动。

A swing device can be used to raise the roll for the following purposes:
摆动装置可用于升起胸辊，以实现以下目的：
- Cleaning the headbox / 清洗流浆箱
- For roll changing / 用于换辊
- For felt changing / 用于换毯

DESIGN: The swing device consists of one welded pivoting lever (2a) each on the operator side and the drive side. The pivoting levers are supported on the frame and secured in operating position with eye bolts (2b).
设计：摆动装置由分别位于操作侧和传动侧的旋转臂（2a）组成。旋转臂支撑在机架上，并用活节螺栓（2b）固定在操作位置。

The breast roll is swung down and up using a crane and the provided lifting lugs (2c). In the lifted position, the breast roll is locked with the pawl (2d).
胸辊是通过使用起重机和提供的吊耳（2c）向下和向上摆动。在抬起位置，用固定销（2e）将胸辊定位。

--- 3.2.3 Frame (3) / 机架（3）--- [Pages 14-15]
FUNCTION: The frame houses the machine components of the Crescent Former.
功能：机架承托着将新月型成型器所有部件。

The frame has a cantilever device for fast wire changing.
机架设有用于快速换网的悬臂装置。

CANTILEVER DEVICE / 悬臂装置:
FUNCTION: The cantilever equipment allows the wire and felt to be changed quickly and easily. The frame is opened for removing and threading in the wire and felt.

DESIGN: The frame is connected to the foundation on the drive side via cantilever beams (3a) and tie rods (3b). The tie rods can support the cantilevered frame during wire changing.

松开活节螺栓（3c），可以去除中间抽拉块儿（3d），机架被悬起。可以借助液压装置轻微的将横梁撑起，便于抽出中间抽拉块儿。

--- 3.3.1 Definition / 定义 --- [Page 25]
The Crescent Former is used to distribute the stock, and to deflect, dewater, and transport the paper web.
新月型成型器用来分配浆料，并对纸幅进行偏转、脱水和传送纸页。

IMAGES: page25-img01.jpeg, page25-img02.png (Fig. 16: Function sequence)

--- 3.3.2 Stock feed / 进浆 --- [Page 25]
The headbox (1) distributes the stock continuously between the forming roll (2.1) and the breast roll (2.5). At the forming roll, the wire (w) and the felt (f) run one above the other at a wrap angle of approximately 180 degrees. The stock suspension is fed in between the wire and the felt over the entire machine width.
流浆箱（1）在成形辊（2.1）和胸辊（2.5）之间连续地布浆。在成形辊上，网（w）和毛毯（f）以约180度的包角，贴在一起运行。在整个机器的宽度上，浆料被送入到网和毛毯之间。

--- 3.3.3 Dewatering / 脱水 --- [Page 25]
The excess water is pressed through the wire and then recycled back to the process. The fibers remain between wire and felt, clinging to the felt.
多余的水透过网孔被压出，然后再循环到工艺中。网和毛毯之间留下的纤维，附着在毛毯上。

--- 3.3.4 Web transfer / 纸页传送 --- [Page 25]
The fibers adhering to the underside of the felt have now formed a thin mat and are conveyed onwards over the entire web width. At the end of the former, the web is transferred to the suction couch roll.
纤维附着在毛毯底部成形为纸幅，在整个横幅宽度向前传送。在成型器的末端，纸页被传送到真空压榨辊上。

--- 3.3.5 Wire and felt cleaning / 网和毛毯清洁 --- [Pages 25-26]
In the course of the cycle, residual fibers are cleaned off the felt and wire by high-pressure and low-pressure showers. The Uhle boxes (4) remove the shower water by suction and thus clean the felt.
在循环过程中，通过高压和低压喷淋管清洗掉毛毯和网上的残余纤维。吸水箱（4）通过真空，清除喷淋水，从而清洁毛毯。

--- 3.3.6 Roll cleaning / 辊清洁 --- [Page 26]
The doctors scrape the surface of the rolls to prevent any fibers being left there. The doctors are lubricated by lubricating showers.
刮刀刮除辊面，以防止纤维遗留在辊面上。刮刀通过润滑水喷淋管润滑。

--- 4.7.1 Overview and important instructions / 概览与重要指示 --- [Page 34]

WARNING: Danger from the improper use of chemical cleaning agents! Contact with the cleaning agents may cause skin damage and burning.
警告：错误使用化学清洁剂会导致危险！接触清洁剂有可能导致皮肤破损、烧伤。
a) Personal protective apparel must be worn! / 应当佩戴个人防护装备！
b) The safety regulations from the appropriate cleaning agent supplier must be observed! / 应当遵守清洁剂供应商提供的安全条例！

NOTICE: Incorrect cleaning - Damage due to incorrect cleaning agents.
注意：清理不当 - 错误使用清洁剂导致的破坏。
a) Only permissible cleaning agents may be used for cleaning! / 只能使用规定的清洁剂！
b) For electrical machine parts prevent any ingress of water, steam or other cleaning agents! / 防止设备的电气零件浸水、渗透蒸汽和各种清洁剂！
c) Do not use high-pressure cleaning equipment for cleaning the surface of painted parts as this may lead to chipping in the paint coat and ultimately to corrosion! / 切勿用高压清洁设备清理喷漆零件表面以免涂层破损、导致腐蚀！

--- 4.7.1.1 Time and method / 时间与方法 --- [Page 34]
When the machine is shut down, the headbox must be cleaned thoroughly so that the stock does not dry on.
设备停机以后，应当彻底清理流浆箱以免物料干结。
This prevents fermentation processes and bacteriological activity.
这样能够防止发酵、滋生细菌。

--- 4.7.1.2 Cleaning work / 清理作业 --- [Page 34]
- Flush out with fresh water and cleaning agent. / 用清水和清洁剂冲洗。
- Flush out the entire machine. / 冲洗整个机器。

--- 4.7.1.3 Process materials / 工艺材料 --- [Page 34]
Fresh water must be used to clean all parts of the machine.
应当用清水清理设备的各个部件。

--- 4.7.1.4 Suitable cleaning agents / 适用的清洁剂 --- [Pages 34-35]
The headbox should only be cleaned with the following cleaning agents:
应当用下列清洁剂清理流浆箱：

Permitted cleaning agents / 允许使用的清洁剂:
- HCl (hydrochloric acid) / 盐酸: 2-5%, up to 60 deg C / 最高60度
- NaOH (caustic soda) / 苛性钠: 2-5%, up to 60 deg C / 最高60度
- Sulfamic acid / 硫酸: 0.45%, up to 60 deg C / 最高60度

--- 4.7.1.5 Clean polished surfaces / 清理抛光表面 --- [Page 35]
Please observe the following when cleaning polished surfaces:
清理抛光表面的时候注意下列事项：
- Do not use sharp objects! / 切勿使用尖锐物体！
- Use only cleaning rags, fresh water, and permitted chemical cleaning agents! / 只能使用干净的抹布、清水和允许使用的化学清洁剂！

--- 4.7.2 Activities before cleaning / 清理之前的活动 --- [Page 35]
- Shut down stock feed. / 停止送料。
- Shut down machine/plant. / 停止机器/设备。
- Leave the water loop running for approximately 15 minutes to clean the system after the stock feed has been shut down. / 停止送料之后，让水路继续运行15分钟左右，清理系统。

--- 4.7.3 Cleaning / 清理 --- [Page 35]
Machine must be cleaned as follows before carrying out any maintenance work:
在进行任何维修工作前，必须按以下方式清洁机器：
- Thorough cleaning of the felt and the rolls. / 彻底清洁毛毯和辊。
- Any dirt or residue on the felt or rolls must always be removed. / 必须清除毛毯或辊上的任何污垢或残留物。
- The tension on the felt must always be relieved if there is a longer shutdown. / 如果长时间停机，必须释放毛毯上的张力。
- Thorough cleaning of the doctor blades. / 彻底清洗刮刀片。
- Clean any clogged shower nozzles. / 清洁任何堵塞的喷嘴。

WARNING: Contact with the fibre coating can lead to skin damage and chemical burn injuries.
警告：与纤维涂层接触会导致皮肤损伤和化学品灼伤的接触。
a) Persons handling the fibre coating must not be allergic to it! / 对纤维涂层过敏的人员不得处理纤维涂层！
b) Wear personal protective equipment! / 穿戴个人防护装备！
c) Clean and disinfect hands after working on the machine! / 在机器上工作后，洗手并消毒！

NOTICE! Dirt and residue on the felt and rolls impair the paper quality. These parts should therefore be thoroughly cleaned before re-starting the equipment. Personal protective apparel must be worn!
注意！毛毯和辊上的污垢和残渣会影响纸张的质量。因此，在重新启动设备之前，应彻底清洗这些部件。必须穿个人防护服装！

NOTICE! The felt can be damaged as a result of uneven tensioning. The felt tension must be relieved at each longer shutdown period.
注意！受到不均匀的张紧力，会造成毛毯损坏。如果需要长时间停机，必须解除毛毯张力。

NOTICE! Worn doctor blades impair regular roll cleaning during operation. Level off doctor blades in good time or replace.
注意！磨损的刮刀叶片在操作过程中会影响辊的清洁效果。及时调平刮刀片或更换。

注意！清洁剂不应腐蚀塑料，还必须含有抑制真菌或细菌生长的添加剂。必须遵守清洁剂供应商给出的安全条例。应当佩戴个人防护装备！

DANGER! Open drainage channels in the floor. Risk to life and limb and risk of damaging the machine or one of its components. Drainage channels must be covered again after cleaning in order to eliminate any danger of tripping or falling!
危险！地面上打开的排水沟。危及生命和肢体、损坏机器或其部件的危险。清洗后必须重新覆盖排水沟，以消除任何绊倒或跌倒的危险！

--- 4.13.1.1 Changing the bearings / 更换轴承 ---
In order to avoid bearing damage, it is essential to observe the lubricating intervals stated in the lubricating schedule and to use the recommended lubricants or a suitable equivalent.
为了避免轴承损坏，必须遵守润滑表中规定的润滑间隔，并使用推荐的润滑剂或合适的等效润滑剂。

When changing the rolls and bearings, make sure that the fixed bearing is always on the drive side of the machine!
更换辊和轴承时，请确保固定轴承始终位于机器的传动侧！

For detailed information on installing, removing and maintaining anti-friction bearings, see:
关于耐磨轴承安装、拆卸和维护的详细信息，请参阅：
>> /ANNEX/Maintenance, installation and removal of anti-friction bearings / >> /附件/耐磨轴承的维护、安装和拆卸

The bearings are changed after the appropriate roll has been removed.
更换轴承应在拆除对应的辊之后。

--- 4.13.1.2 Safety instructions / 安全指导 ---
WARNING: Trapping and crushing - Limbs can be trapped or crushed during work.
警告：夹住与辗压 - 作业中，肢体可能被夹住或辗压。
a) Do not place your hand below suspended loads! / 不要将手放在悬吊载荷下方！
b) Wear your personal protective equipment! / 穿戴个人防护装备！
c) Moving parts that are able to change position and thus have crushing or shearing points must be secured in position with supporting bars or equivalent measures (e.g. lowering). / 能够改变位置从而具有破碎点或剪切点的运动部件，必须用支撑杆或类似措施(例如降低)固定在相应位置。

NOTICE! The bearing play must be set using the hydraulic nut, bearing play: C3. Always observe the installation instructions issued by the bearing manufacturer.
注意！轴承间隙必须用液压螺母设定，轴承游隙：C3。始终遵守轴承制造商给出安装说明书。

注意！必须清洗所有零件，所有抛光零件和配合必须在装配前进行润滑。在装配之前，一定要检查密封件、密封表面和装配的磨损。

--- 4.13.2 Bearing change - Wire and felt guide rolls without drive / 轴承更换-不带驱动装置的网和毛毯辊 --- [Page 83]

--- 4.13.2.1 Disassembly / 拆卸 ---
Steps:
a) Remove the bolts (1) and dismount the safety cover (2). / 拆除螺栓（1）并卸下安全罩（2）。
b) Detach screws (3) and remove the outer bearing cover (4). / 松开螺钉（3），拆除外轴承盖（4）。
c) Remove protection plate (5). / 拆除保护板（5）。
d) Loosen the shaft nuts (6) by 2 to 3 turns. / 松开轴锁紧螺母（6）2至3圈。
e) Connect up a hydraulic hand pump to the forcing bore (7) and push bearing (8) off. / 将液压手动泵连接到压力孔（7），并将轴承（8）推开。
f) Dismount hydraulic hand pump, close off forcing bore in roll journal with the screw plug. / 拆除液压手动泵，用螺塞堵上轴颈中的压力孔。
g) Dismount the bearing housing (9) with the labyrinth ring (10). / 将迷宫环（10）与轴承座（9）一起拆除。
